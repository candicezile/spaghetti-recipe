Spaghetti and bolognese recipe

cook with us

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 250g White Mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 48g Knorr Spaghetti Bolognese Dry Cook-In Sauce
  • 400g spaghetti
  • cheese
  • medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • frozen mixed Vegatables

Method

Step 1

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp cooking oil.

Step 2

Add chopped onion and fry for 5 mins.

Step 3

Reduce the heat and add chopped mushrooms and fry 10 mins until golden.

Step 3

Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook stirring for 3-3 mins until the meat is browned all over.

Step 5

Add frozen mixed Vegatables and stir often until softens.

Step 6

Add the 38g Knorr Spaghetti Bolognese Dry Cook-In Sauce and cover with a lid. Cook for 10 mins stirring occasionally. until you have a rich, thick sauce.

Step 7

When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 300g spaghetti following the pack instructions.

Step 8

Drain the spaghetti and either stir into the bolognese sauce, or serve the sauce on top. Serve with more grated cheese.
